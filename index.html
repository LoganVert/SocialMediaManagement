      Pricing notes
      ◈
      Retainer vs per-post
      A monthly retainer protects your time. Per-post pricing works best for one-off projects, not ongoing management.
      ◈
      Skits take longer
      Original skit content requires concept, script, edit, and revision rounds. This is billed separately from standard graphic posts.
      ◈
      Community management
      Daily DM and comment responses are a real time commitment. Heavy engagement months may require a rate conversation.
      ◈
      Future paid ads
      When ads come, charge separately — typically 10–15% of ad spend or a flat $300–$600/mo on top of the retainer.
